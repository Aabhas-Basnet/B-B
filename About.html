About us

Established in the lord's year of 2015 on the georgian calendar and as of 2072 on the vikrami calendar, Baibhavi and baibhav builders, an "A" class certified construction company, proudly celebrates 5 years of serving our community with construction expertise on buildings and infrastructures based on throughout the country of Nepal. Founded by chairperson Mr.Dipendra Karki initially as "D" class construction company and starting off with projects on buildings sector, our organization has expanded its expertise and resources serving infrastructure development primarily but not limited to construction of highway projects. As a prospering company, we would also like to announce our expansion of construction works on various other infrastructure developemt projects including Retaining Structures, Bridges, Irrigation Canals and River Training Structures.

B&B is one of the most well-known construction firms in the country. This construction company designs, finances, builds, and runs infrastructure and facilities with the best quality, speed, and care in the most cost-effective method possible. It  has demonstrated its capacity to execute its task - regardless of how large, difficult, or distant the project, B&B Construction has demonstrated expertise over the previous 5 years. It establishes a precedent in Nepal by shifting from monetary contracts to project development. Through collaboration with local and foreign stakeholders, B&B hopes to win and promote various infrastructure development projects. Today, B&B Builders, one of Nepal's A-grade construction businesses, thrives in the construction industry because of its skills in leadership, technique, engineering, technology, collaboration, and logistics.
